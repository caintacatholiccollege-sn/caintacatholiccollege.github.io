Admission

Admission at CCC

Admission of students is a sole prerogative of the Institution. Hence,
the school reserves the right to admit or reject any student seeking
admission in the school. However, a student may only be admitted
after he/she has accomplished/submitted the following requirements:

Basic Education Department

List of Requirements:
a. Report Card (F-138) from the school last attended (Original & photocopy)
b. NSO Birth Certificate (Original & photocopy)
c. Baptismal Certificate (Original & photocopy)
d. Two (2) 1 x 1 ID colored pictures
e. Certification of passing the Entrance Examination
f. Certification of Good Moral Character from the school last attended (Original & photocopy)
g. 1 Long Folder
h. Two hundred Pesos (Php200.00) Entrance Fee
i. Appearance of pupil applicants for oral interview

College Department

List of Requirments: a. Report Card (F-138) with at least 80% general average
b. NSO Birth Certificate (Original & photocopy)
c. Baptismal Certificate (Original & photocopy)
d. Two (2) recent 2 x 2 ID colored pictures
e. Php200.00 Testing Fee
f. Certification of Good Moral Character from an authorized school official (Original & photocopy)
g. If married, marriage certificate issued by the Catholic Church
h. Personal appearance of College applicants for oral interview
i. Shall meet the specific requirements for the designated course applied for.
